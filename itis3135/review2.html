Detailed Review

Basics

  • Link leads to the correct page.
  • No issues with file names.

Overall Design

  • Everything is well spaced out, not cluttered together.
  • Style for the webpages are the same and easily seen and contrasted with one another.
  • All content on the page are designed similarly or the same as each other.
  • All images and text elements are aligned with each other.
  • Proximity between all pages are the same, no problems found.

Content of the Website

  • All pages have headers.
  • Pages have mains.
  • Pages have footers.
  • Pages have a navigation element.
  • The home seems to have no content aside from the header and the small introduction to the site.

Headers

  • The client's name is clearly seen with an H1.
  • All headers are styled consistantly and used where they should be.
  • There was no H2 detailing what the Statistic and History tab lead to.

Main

  • All mains start with an H2, save for the Statistic and History.
  • There is an image that isn't showing in the Real Madrid Home tab near the bottom.
  • There is another image that isn't showing at the FC Barcelona tab at the bottom.
  • Information is presented well and easily seen to read.Good contrast all around.

Footer Content

  • There is a footer and validation buttons, but no Accumulus.
  • Good information about who designed the website and aligned together well.

Assignment Specifics
