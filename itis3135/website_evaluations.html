Website Evaulations

YouTube

This website is well designed for the user. Used for entertainment and shows videos on various topics ranging from children videos and animations to educational videos, animations, and personalized entertainment.

  • C: Contrast of the website is done well. In either mobile or pc, text is usually easy to see and understand. Links are clearly defined and apparent.
  • R: Repetition of the website is done well also. Everything is done in a way to make sure that the user knows where to click and what they mean and go to.
  • A: Alignment of the website is done well. The alignment is usually centered or aligned to the right if seeing a video. Videos are seen clearly in the middle and large enough to read.
  • P: Proximity of the website is done well. Everything is spaced out enough to not feel that everything is close together. Also seperated by tags, lines, or genres of the videos to make it even more clear.
